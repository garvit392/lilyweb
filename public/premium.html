FAQ

›Why crypto only?

It keeps fees low, removes chargebacks, and means we never store your card details. We accept LTC today and are adding more coins soon.

›Is the lifetime plan really lifetime?

›How do I claim premium after paying?

Yes — once every 30 days, ask us in the support server.

›Refund policy?

Crypto payments are non-refundable, but if something genuinely doesn't work we'll make it right. Talk to us first in support.
