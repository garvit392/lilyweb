Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This policy explains what data Lily collects, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and the choices you have. We collect the minimum needed to operate the bot and dashboard.

1. Data we collect

1.1 From Discord OAuth login (dashboard)

When you click "Login with Discord" on the dashboard, Discord shares the following with us using the identify and guilds scopes:

FieldWhy we use it
Discord user IDIdentify your session
Username and global display nameShow "logged in as …" in the dashboard
Avatar hashDisplay your avatar in the dashboard
List of servers (guilds) you are in, with your permissions and ownershipShow only the servers you can manage in the server picker
OAuth access & refresh tokensMaintain your session; never shown to anyone else

We do NOT request your email address, your messages, your friends list, or any payment information.

1.2 From server activity (bot)

When Lily is in a server, it processes Discord events strictly as needed to deliver the features that server has enabled. Examples:

  • Moderation / Antinuke / Automod — message content, attachments, member join/leave events, role/channel changes, audit-log entries are inspected to detect rule violations or nuke attempts. Detected violations may be stored as case records (offender ID, moderator ID, reason, timestamp).
  • Welcomer — new-member events trigger a welcome message; nothing about the message itself is retained.
  • Tickets — channel IDs, ticket-opener IDs, and (optionally) chat transcripts are stored for the lifetime of the ticket / configured retention.
  • Logs — when this module is enabled by a server admin, message edits/deletions, member joins/leaves, voice events, and role updates are forwarded to the channels chosen by that admin. We do not duplicate this data on our servers beyond what's needed to deliver the message.
  • Music / Playlist — track titles and saved playlists are stored against your Discord user ID.
  • Custom roles, J2C, Giveaways, Autoresponder, Server Config — only the configuration you provide (role IDs, channel IDs, prefixes, colors, etc.) is stored.

1.3 From the dashboard (in-browser)

Your toggle states and field values entered in the dashboard are stored in your browser's localStorage under the key lily.dashboard.v1. They never leave your device unless and until you press a "Save to bot" action that explicitly synchronises a value.

1.4 Operational logs

Like any web service, our servers produce request logs containing IP address, timestamp, request path, status code and user-agent. These are kept for up to 14 days for abuse-prevention and debugging, then deleted.

3. How we use your data

  • To deliver the features each server has enabled.
  • To keep the bot, dashboard, and your account secure (rate-limit abuse, detect raids, etc.).
  • To debug errors and improve reliability.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it to build cross-service profiles.

4. Who we share data with

  • Discord — by definition, all bot traffic flows through Discord's APIs and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy.
  • Hosting / infrastructure providers — used to run the bot and website. These providers process data on our behalf under their own confidentiality terms.
  • We may disclose information when legally required by a valid subpoena or court order.

5. Your rights and choices

  • Access / correction / deletion — you may request a copy of, or deletion of, the data tied to your Discord user ID by contacting us in the support server. We will action verified requests within 30 days.
  • Revoke OAuth — at any time you may revoke our access from your Discord Authorised Apps page.
  • Remove the bot — server owners can kick Lily at any time, which stops all event processing for that server.
  • Clear local settings — clearing your browser site data removes the lily.dashboard.v1 entry instantly.

6. Cookies

We set a single first-party session cookie named lily.sid when you log in to the dashboard. It is HTTP-only, SameSite=Lax, and contains only a randomly generated session identifier. We do not use third-party tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

7. Children

Lily is not directed at children under the minimum age set by Discord for your jurisdiction (generally 13, or 16 in parts of the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone below that age. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it.

8. International transfers

Our servers may be located outside your country. By using Lily you consent to your data being processed in jurisdictions that may have different data-protection laws than your own.

9. Security

We use HTTPS, modern session encryption, and least-privilege Discord scopes. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your data and to disclose any verified breach without undue delay.

10. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, post a notice in the support server.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, reach us in the official support server.
